TURMERIC

Strong herb for reducing inflammation, aiding digestion, and supporting joint health.

High doses may cause side effects like stomach upset.


CINNAMON

Contains antioxidants; some studies suggest it may help regulate blood sugar, reduce inflammation, or improve cholesterol levels.


MORINGA

It can boost immunity, reduce inflammation, improve digestion, and support skin and hair health. Some studies suggest potential benefits for blood sugar and cholesterol regulation, but evidence is preliminary and mixed.

High doses may cause side effects like nausea or interact with medications.


CURRY

This includes a mix of spices.
  • Turmeric: Reduce inflammation and offer antioxidant support, potentially aiding joint and brain health.
  • Cinnamon: Help regulate blood sugar and provide antioxidants.
  • Fenugreek: Improve digestion and support blood sugar control.
  • Cumin: Enhance digestion and offer antioxidant properties.
  • Coriander: Aid digestion and provide antioxidants.
  • Paprika: Contains vitamin A and antioxidants, supports eye health.
  • Cayenne: Boost metabolism and circulation, with anti-inflammatory potential.
  • Cardamom: Aid digestion and freshen breath.
  • Cloves: Have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects.
  • Nutmeg: Support digestion and sleep, in small amounts.
